SAVEMODE

Water & Energy Saving Technology


Inspired by nature, designed by intelligence. Alder is committed to saving energy and water to protect the planet with their SAVEMODE innovation. With SAVEMODE when you turn on your mixer tap from the neutral position, you use only 100% cold water. Whereas conventional mixers use 50% hot and 50% cold, thus igniting the hot water system and using unwanted energy. Alders innovative SAVEMODE reduces your use of energy by not igniting the hot water system when it is not required

LEAD FREE

Delivering Lead Free Tapware


HUMMAN’s is delivering important public health benefits to our builders, their customers and the community in 2024, with the introduction of lead free tapware at the builder specification and value for money end of the market. With a focus on wellness and living better for a more sustainable life, Alder have focused on delivering lead free products first across their volume products. All of Alder’s current tapware range has used on average less than half the allowable lead under the current Australian Standards. The company has always worked hard to minimise the use of lead.

UNIVERSAL IN-WALL BODY

Simplification and
choice


HUMMAN Universal is the universal in-wall body system that fits Vito Bertoni products, regardless of colour finish. Intelligently designed with the handle lever pointing upwards, downwards or progressive function, the innovation offers ultimate flexibility by allowing the colour finish to be interchanged pre or post installation, eliminating costly hold ups and rectification work by enabling easy updates to the finish at any time. To simplify the ALDER Universal required based on the products handle design they are colour coded for easy identification.
